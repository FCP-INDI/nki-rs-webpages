NIDA
-----

This measure from the National Institute on Drug Abuse is a brief self-report questionnaire comprised of 8 items assessing use of cannabis, cocaine, amphetamine-type stimulants, inhalants, sedatives, hallucinogens, opioids and other miscellaneous drugs, alcohol, and tobacco. 

**Domains Assessed:** :doc:`Substance Use <substance>`

**Note:** The NIDA is administered to children and adults ages 12-85.

**References:** WHO ASSIST Working Group, 2002
