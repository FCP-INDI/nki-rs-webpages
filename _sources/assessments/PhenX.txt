PhenX Sexual History
---------------------

The PhenX provides an objective assessment of self-reported sexual
behaviors.

**Domains Assessed:** :doc:`Sex and Gender* <sexgender>`

**Note:** The PhenX is administered to adolescents and adults ages
13-85.

**More Information:** `*PhenX Tool Kit* <https://www.phenxtoolkit.org/index.php?pageLink=browse.protocoldetails&id=101401>`_
