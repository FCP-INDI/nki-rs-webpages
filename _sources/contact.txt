Contact
=======

The Rockland Sample maintains a repository containing frequently asked questions about the dataset and their answers `here <https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/nkirs_forum>`_.  If a question you have is not answered there, or you would like further clarification do not hesitate to contact us at `rocklandsample@nki.rfmh.org <mailto:rocklandsample%40nki.rfmh.org>`_.
