Help and Frequently Asked Questions
------------------------------------

The Rockland Sample maintains a repository containing frequently asked questions about the dataset and their answers `here <https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/nkirs_forum>`_.  If a question you have is not answered there, or you would like further clarification do not hesitate to contact John Pellman, the Rockland Sample liaison at `john.pellman@childmind.org <mailto:john.pellman%40childmind.org>`_.
